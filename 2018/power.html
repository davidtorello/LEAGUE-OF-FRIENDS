Week 4

Week 3 is in the books, and with it another round of profound disappointments and surprise performances. We're starting to see the eschelons separate in the league tables... the air up here is quite nice and I highly suggest giving it a breathe. The Equalizer and The Commish sit at the top in tier 1, and the margin is healthy between the lofty peaks of fantasy valhalla and the tier 2 teams, who are kind of like the waitstaff for our feasting halls. Then again, it's only one loss of difference, kind of like a potential "Red Wedding" scenario, so complacency means doom. Nortley, Giardino's Saint Stack, and AI Katz have a pretty substantial grip on the next wrung down... who I guess are like the peasants that wash the laundry in this heirarchy. Except that tier 3 is just Haupt, so the laundry is going to take a while. Tier 4 are akin to the bratty children of the laundry maids - helpful, but mostly in the way and always making trouble. DW, Pat Rick, and Baldwin can do a thing to anyone, but just haven't done it yet. And then below that would be the tier 5 mud farmers, who farm mud. AJ and Callie are the dirtybois.

Dirty, dirt bois.
